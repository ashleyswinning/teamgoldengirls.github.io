Synopsis of Project

Drinking water is key to living a healthy lifestyle, but it can be difficult to keep up with how much water is consumed daily. It is also difficult to know how much water to drink based off of body weight and physical activity levels.

We have chosen to improve Plant Nanny, an already existing app that is used to track water intake. Although the idea behind Plant Nanny is fantastic, the app itself is outdated and is not very accessible to a wide range of users. We hope to create a better version of this app that accommodates a larger target audience and provides a richer user experience.
